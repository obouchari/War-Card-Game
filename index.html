Not Stars War, It's Cards War!

Let's Play

Rules

  1. Each player will be dealt 26 cards each (half of the deck). Cards facing down.
  2. Both players flip one card at the same time, who has the higher value wins. That person gets to take both cards, putting them aside.
  3. Keep repeating step 2. If both players flip a card of equal value, the WAR begins! Both players take another card and put it facing down on top of the first card, then take a third card and flip it face up. The player with the higher value card wins all six cards on the table.
  4. If the two new cards are also the same rank, it's WAR again. Repeat step 3 until one of the players has a card of higher value.
  5. First player who finishes all their cards will lose the game.

Notes:

  • Cards are ranked with the ace as the highest and two as the lowest.
  • There is no strategy for War since the game is based on chance.
  • If a player finishes all their cards during a war, he/she loses immediately.
